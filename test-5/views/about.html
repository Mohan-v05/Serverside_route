POWER UP YOUR FITNESS WITH MaxFit Gym

Our vision is to bring our fitness centres to you: We currently operate a chain of 24 fitness centres, serving over 25,000 members in Colombo and its suburbs. Moreover, with over 100,000 sq ft of gym space, Power World offers an expansive and versatile environment for a comprehensive fitness experience.

We believe that fitness is a lifestyle. Our passion for fitness and commitment to our members sets us apart from other gyms. Our trainers are certified experts in their field and they will work with you to create a personalized fitness plan based on your goals. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, we have the tools and knowledge to help you succeed.
